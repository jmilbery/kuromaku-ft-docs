# Kuromaku Fit-Trak

A simple, private workout tracker for iOS. No accounts, no subscriptions, no data collection — just you and your workouts.

## About the App

Kuromaku Fit-Trak tracks **30 exercises** across three categories (Strength Training, Cardio, Stretching), daily water intake, and provides a **score-based color-coded calendar** to visualize your consistency.

- **No accounts** — Open the app and start tracking immediately
- **No servers** — All data stays on your device
- **No subscriptions** — Free, no ads, no in-app purchases

## Features

- **Score-Based Calendar** — Days colored by daily score across 5 tiers: Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum
- **30 Exercises, 3 Categories** — Strength training (with weight), cardio (with minutes), and stretching
- **Water Tracker** — 8 tappable cups per day with timestamps and monthly totals
- **Exercise Notes** — Add quick notes to any exercise (up to 100 characters)
- **Monthly Summary** — Workout days, strength/cardio/stretch counts, total water at a glance
- **CSV Export** — Full workout data export for Excel, Numbers, or Google Sheets
- **Smart Reminders** — Up to 3 gentle daily reminders that stop when you log a workout
- **Rename Exercises** — Customize any exercise name to fit your routine
- **3 Color Palettes** — Choose from Steel Blue, Blue + Gold, or Warm Maroon themes

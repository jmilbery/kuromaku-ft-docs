# Privacy Policy

**Kuromaku Fit-Trak by Kuromaku** | Effective February 13, 2026

## Overview

Kuromaku Fit-Trak is a workout tracking app that respects your privacy. We believe your fitness data is personal and should stay on your device.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** Kuromaku Fit-Trak does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage data, or analytics. The app has no accounts and no network connectivity required.

## Data Storage

All workout data you enter is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes:

- Workout sessions and dates
- Exercise entries (sets, reps, weight, minutes)
- Per-exercise notes
- Custom exercise names
- Water intake records

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it using the in-app CSV export feature.

## Third-Party Services

Kuromaku Fit-Trak does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking technologies.

## Notifications

Kuromaku Fit-Trak may request permission to send local notifications for workout reminders. These notifications are generated entirely on your device — no data is sent to any server. You can enable or disable reminders at any time in the app's Settings. The app only requests alert permission (no sound).

## Data Export

Kuromaku Fit-Trak includes a CSV export feature that allows you to save your workout data to a file. When you use this feature, the file is generated on your device and shared only through the destinations you choose (such as Files, AirDrop, or email). We have no access to the exported data.

## Children's Privacy

Kuromaku Fit-Trak does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

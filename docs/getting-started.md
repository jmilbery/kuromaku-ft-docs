# Getting Started

## First Launch

When you open Kuromaku Fit-Trak for the first time, you'll see the **calendar view**. There's no account to create — you're ready to start tracking immediately.

## Recording Your First Workout

1. Tap **today's date** on the calendar
2. Tap the **"Record Workout"** button
3. Use the **dropdown menus** to add exercises from any category
4. **Check off sets** left to right (checkboxes enforce sequential order)
5. Enter **weight** (for strength) or **minutes** (for cardio) as needed
6. Optionally tap the **note icon** to add a quick note to any exercise
7. Tap **"Return to Calendar"** to save your workout

Your calendar day will now be color-coded based on your daily score.

## Understanding the Calendar

The calendar is your home screen and shows your workout history at a glance. Each day is scored based on your workout activity across categories and water intake, then colored by tier:

| Tier | Score Range | Description |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| No Workout | 0 | No shading |
| Bronze | 1 - 30 | Light activity |
| Silver | 31 - 60 | Moderate activity |
| Gold | 61 - 90 | Strong workout day |
| Platinum | 91+ | Outstanding effort |

The **selected day** shows a black circle outline. Only **today and yesterday** can be edited.

You can choose from three color palettes in **Settings** to personalize the calendar appearance.

## Navigation

The app has three tabs at the bottom:

- **Workouts** — Calendar view, the main screen
- **Export** — Export your data as CSV
- **Settings** — Reminders, theme, exercise management, app info

## Water Tracking

Below the calendar legend, you'll see **8 cup icons**. Tap them left to right to log glasses of water for the selected day. The cups are only editable for today and yesterday.

## Monthly Summary

Below the water tracker, the **Monthly Summary** card shows five stats for the displayed month:
- **Days** — Total workout days
- **Strength** — Total strength exercises logged
- **Cardio** — Total cardio exercises logged
- **Stretch** — Total stretching exercises logged
- **Water** — Total water glasses
